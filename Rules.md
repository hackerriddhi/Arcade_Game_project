# Game Rules & Controls

###  Snake and Fruit
- **Controls:** Use `W`, `A`, `S`, `D` to move.
- **Objective:** Eat the 'F' (Fruit) to grow. Your score increases with every fruit.
- **Game Over:** Touching the borders (`*`) or your own body (`o`) ends the game.

###  Tic-Tac-Toe
- **Controls:** Enter the row and column numbers (1 to 3) when prompted (e.g., `1 2`).
- **Objective:** Get three 'O's in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
- **AI:** Be careful! The bot is programmed to block your wins and try to win itself.

###  Minesweeper
- **Controls:** - `R x y`: Reveal a tile at coordinates x, y.
  - `F x y`: Flag a tile where you suspect a mine.
  - `B`: Go back to the main menu.
- **Objective:** Flag all 10 mines or reveal all safe tiles.
- **Game Over:** Selecting a tile that contains a mine (`X`).
